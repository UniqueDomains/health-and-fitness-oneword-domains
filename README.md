## ⚠️ Methodology and caveats

This selection is broad by design: 22,037 domains tied to health and fitness keywords, spread across 505 TLDs. The sample names show that many are keyword-led rather than pure one-word brands, and extension quality varies widely. For founders, the main question is whether the full name reads credibly enough to support a real brand without creating confusion. For investors, the key test is whether the ask leaves room for a reasonable spread after accounting for extension strength and likely renewal drag. With a median ask near $703, the better candidates are usually the ones where the keyword and TLD make sense together, not just the cheapest listings.

- Check whether the keyword and TLD fit naturally together
- Use the $703 median ask as a rough pricing anchor
- Favor memorable names over awkward keyword-extension combos
- Watch niche TLDs for weaker trust and renewal risk
